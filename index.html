Who We Are

Thank you for visiting our site. We are Bombay Groove: London’s specialist Bollywood String Quartet. Our group comprises two violinists, a viola player and a ‘cellist, and we have been performing Bollywood and Western music together since 2010. We are all conservatoire-trained professional musicians who perform at the highest level all around the world.

What We Do

We provide beautiful music for your event. As wedding specialists, we are available to play for all parts of the celebration – including the ceremony (religious or civil) and reception. Or, for your engagement or Mehndi party. We’re also available to play at corporate events and other celebrations, and although we’re based in London, UK, we are available to perform worldwide.

Prices

Our prices are dependent on the location of your event, and how long you would like us to play for. However, currently we charge £750 for a two hour set in the Greater London area. Additional hours are £125 per hour. This price includes our own amplification (500 WATTS) and as we are not VAT registered, there is none to pay.
